Rajam Green Granite:

The rugged terrains of Rajam in Srikakulam District feature the distinctive Dyke of Rajam Green Granite . This unique type, design, and pattern are exclusive to the Rajam area and cannot be found anywhere else.

Rajam Green Granite is known for its elegant green tones interlaced with lighter veins, offering a sophisticated look for any architectural or interior design. Its resilience against wear and tear makes it ideal for countertops, flooring, and wall claddings. This granite brings a natural, refreshing feel to any space, making it a popular choice among homeowners and designers alike.

  • High Durability & Strength
  • Resistant to Heat & Stains
  • Ideal for Indoor & Outdoor Use
  • Elegant & Timeless Appeal

Rajam Green Granite is widely used in commercial and residential spaces due to its robust nature and aesthetic appeal. The rich green hues add a natural charm, making it a great option for enhancing the beauty of interiors and exteriors alike.
